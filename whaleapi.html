The Orca Tracking App

For Orca Enthusiasts

See the whales without the tanks

Orcas are among the most intelligent animals on the planet and have captivated us for centuries. With their family-oriented and matriachal based societies, they are likely to be seen in large groups or pods. These animals are extremely playful, engaging in activities such as breaching, spyhopping and tail slapping! This app has been created to show the world that these animals are accessible to observe without the need of tanks and concrete slabs.

Click a whale on the map to see what people have submitted about their sightings!

All data has been retrieved from The Whale Museum's API. This data is coming from people who have spotted the whales in the wild and provided details including but not limited to coordinates, how many were spotted and the location.
